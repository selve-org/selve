## Your Privacy Matters

Here's the thing: we know sharing personality stuff can feel vulnerable. That's why we built in some privacy features:

### What People CAN See:
- Your dimension scores (LUMEN, VARA, CHRONOS, etc.)
- Your personality narrative (the story about your profile)
- Your core identity traits
- General demographics (age range, that kind of thing)

### What People CANNOT See:
- Your email address
- Your exact location
- Your individual question answers
- Any identifying personal information

Think of it like showing someone your personality "highlight reel" without giving them access to your entire profile.

## Making Your Results Private Again

Changed your mind? No problem. Here's how to take your results offline:

1. Go back to your results page
2. Click the **Share** button again
3. Hit **"Make Private"**

Done! Your old link stops working immediately. Anyone who tries to visit it will just see a "not found" message.

### What Happens to the Old Link?

When you make your results private, we don't just hide themâ€”we actually **delete the share link completely**. That means:

- The old link is permanently dead (no one can access it anymore)
- If you share again later, you get a **brand new link**
- You can't accidentally "resurrect" old links

This is by design. We figured you'd want a fresh start each time you decide to share, rather than worrying about old links floating around.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can people see my answers to individual questions?**  
Nope! They only see your final dimension scores and narrative. Your raw responses stay private.

**Q: Will the link expire after a certain time?**  
Not unless you make it private. Once shared, the link works forever (or until you revoke it).

**Q: Can I share different results with different people?**  
Each share link shows your current results. If you want to show different results to different people, you'd need to take the assessment multiple times (which... actually might be interesting for seeing how you change over time!).

**Q: What if I shared my results but then got a better score?**  
There's no "better" score in personality assessments! But if you retake the test and want to share updated results, just make the old link private and create a new one with your fresh scores.

## The Technical Stuff (For the Curious)

For those who want to know what's happening under the hood:

When you create a shareable link, we:
1. Generate a random, cryptographically secure ID (it's basically impossible to guess)
2. Mark your results as "public" in our database
3. Create a special URL that only shows your scores and narrative (not your personal info)

When you make it private, we:
1. Mark your results as "not public"
2. Delete the share ID completely
3. Any future visits to that link get a 404 error

Your data stays secure either wayâ€”we just change who can see your personality profile.
